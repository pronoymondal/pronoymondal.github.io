Personal Stuff

I have grown up and been educated at India.

During my undergraduate, I decided to be a researcher. I became interested in Biostatistics after I worked on a summer project with Prof. Indranil Mukhopdhyay in 2012. In that project, i worked on finding asymptotic distribution of KBAT statistic (Mukhopadhyay et al. (2009)). During my Ph.D. I was introduced to the problem of RNA-seq data analysis and I became fascinated to work on Computational Biology.
